Alien Signal - Ludum Dare 59

# Short Description

You are a young cadet at a guntower in charge of protecting earth from alien invaders.

After months without action you start to pickup some strange signals on your radar.

Defend your home!

# Default Controls
## Keyboard:

Movement: A/D or Arrow Keys

Fire: Space or Cntrl or Enter
## Controller:

Movement: JoyStick 

Fire: Y
